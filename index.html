The Gauntlet Series Founders

The Gauntlet Series Soccer Tournament was founded in 2017 by former Chippewa High School varsity soccer players. We wanted to play soccer and the best way to do that was to host a mini tournament and invite our friends. Little did we know that the tournament would turn into an annual event. It's been exciting meeting new players and watching teams from different backgrounds compete against one another. We are also very fortunate to learn about business, leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving through this tournament.
